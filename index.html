E-Commerce Customer Analysis

An analysis of 3,800+ customer records to understand purchasing behavior, revenue patterns, and subscription trends.

Problem

The business lacked clarity on customer behavior, discount effectiveness, and subscription impact on revenue.

Data

Customer demographic data, product categories, purchase history, review ratings, discount usage, shipping type, and subscription status.

Data Cleaning & Preparation

Handled missing values, removed unnecessary columns, standardized categories, and prepared the dataset using Python (Pandas and NumPy) for analysis.

Analysis & SQL Queries

  • Compared revenue by gender
  • Identified high-spending discount users
  • Analyzed average spend by shipping type
  • Evaluated revenue by subscription status
  • Segmented customers into New, Returning, and Loyal groups

Insights

Subscription customers generated higher lifetime revenue, express shipping users spent more on average, and a small set of products accounted for a large portion of discounted purchases.

Outcome

Built an interactive Power BI dashboard and a presentation to communicate insights clearly to non-technical stakeholders.
